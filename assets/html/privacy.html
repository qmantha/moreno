Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines the information we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

  • User IDs: Used to identify users and personalize bot responses.
  • Guild IDs: Used to store settings and preferences for each server.
  • Role IDs: Used for role-based permissions and features.
  • Channel IDs: Used to manage bot interactions within specific channels.
  • Past Usernames and Nicknames: Collected under the namehistory feature to track name changes.
  • Other information necessary for the bot's functionality.

2. How We Use This Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide and maintain our bot's services.
  • Improve user experience and personalize interactions.
  • Ensure compliance with server-specific rules and permissions.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored securely and is not shared with any third parties.

4. Data Retention

We retain data as long as it is necessary to provide our services. You can request data removal by contacting us.

5. Changes to This Policy
