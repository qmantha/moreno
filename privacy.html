Wonder

Privacy Policy

1. Information collection and use

Wonder does not collect any personal information from users.

2. Log Data

Like many other bot services, Wonder collects log data that includes the following information:

  • Guild IDs
  • Guild Names
  • Channel IDs
  • Role IDs
  • User IDs
  • Message Timestamps
  • Message IDs
  • Nicknames and Usernames
  • Message content when a command is run (stored for a max of 7 days) or when arguments are passed for commands
  • Last message edited history (stored for a max of 2 hours or less)
  • Last message deleted history (stored for a max of 2 hours or less)
  • Last reaction remove on message content (stored for a max of 2 hours or less)

3. Why do we need the data and how is it used?

When a command is invoked, we store message content for up to 7 days for debugging purposes.

Guild IDs, Channel IDs, Role IDs, User IDs, and Message IDs are stored for system operations to aggregate data.

Nickname and Username changes are logged to enable the "namehistory" command. Users can clear this data themselves at any time.

Guild name changes are logged to enable the "guildnicknames" command. Server administrators can clear this data themselves at any time.

4. Who is your collected information shared with?

We do not sell or expose your information to others or third parties by any means.

5. Security

Wonder takes reasonable measures to protect user data, but no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Wonder may update its Privacy Policy from time to time. Users will be notified of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Continuing to use our services after any changes indicates agreement with these terms. Violations of our terms of service may result in a permanent ban from all of our services.

7. Contact Us

Any information we collect is not used maliciously. If any information stated here seems or is misleading, please contact us immediately.
