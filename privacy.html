Privacy Policy

1. Information Collection and Use

Melbot does not collect any personal information from users.

2. Log Data

Like many other bot services, Melbot collects log data that includes the following information:

  • Guild IDs
  • Guild Names
  • Channel IDs
  • Role IDs
  • User IDs
  • Message Timestamps
  • Message IDs
  • Nicknames and Usernames
  • Message Content when a command is run (stored for a max of 3 days) or when arguments are passed for commands

3. Why We Need This Data and How It Is Used

When a command is invoked, we store message content for up to 3 days for debugging purposes.

Guild IDs, Channel IDs, Role IDs, User IDs, and Message IDs are stored for system operations to aggregate data.

Nickname and Username changes are logged to enable the "namehistory" command. Users can clear this data themselves at any time.

Guild name changes are logged to enable the "gnames" command. Server administrators can clear this data themselves at any time.

4. Who Is Your Collected Information Shared With?

We do not sell or expose your information to others or third parties by any means.

5. Security

Melbot takes reasonable measures to protect user data, but no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Melbot may update its Privacy Policy from time to time. Users will be notified of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. Continuing to use our services after any changes indicates agreement with these terms. Violations of our terms of service may result in a permanent ban from all of our services.

7. Contact Us

Any information we collect is not used maliciously. If any information stated here seems or is misleading, please contact us immediately.
